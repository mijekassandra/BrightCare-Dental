Treating gum disease and preserving your natural teeth

Protecting Your Gum Health

At BrightCare Dental, we understand that healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy smile. Our Gum & Periodontal Care services focus on preventing, diagnosing, and treating gum disease to preserve your natural teeth and maintain optimal oral health.

Gum disease, if left untreated, can lead to tooth loss and has been linked to other health issues. Our experienced team provides comprehensive periodontal care, from routine deep cleanings to advanced treatments for severe gum disease.

We use gentle, effective techniques to treat gum disease while ensuring your comfort throughout the process. Our goal is to restore your gum health, prevent further damage, and help you maintain a healthy smile for life.

Our comprehensive gum and periodontal care services

Periodontal Exams

Comprehensive evaluations to assess gum health and detect early signs of periodontal disease.

Scaling & Root Planing

Deep cleaning procedures to remove plaque and tartar from below the gum line and smooth root surfaces.

Periodontal Maintenance

Ongoing care and monitoring to maintain gum health after periodontal treatment.

Antibiotic Therapy

Targeted antibiotic treatments to combat bacterial infections and support healing.

Gum Grafting

Surgical procedures to restore receding gums and protect exposed tooth roots.

Gum Disease Education

Personalized guidance on maintaining healthy gums and preventing gum disease at home.
