General & Preventive Care

Keeping your smile healthy with routine care and early detection

Comprehensive Preventive Dental Care

At BrightCare Dental, we believe that prevention is the foundation of excellent oral health. Our General & Preventive Care services are designed to keep your teeth and gums healthy, catch problems early, and help you maintain a beautiful smile for years to come.

Regular preventive care visits are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. During these appointments, our experienced team performs thorough cleanings, comprehensive exams, and provides personalized guidance to help you care for your smile at home.

We use modern technology and gentle techniques to ensure your comfort throughout every visit. Our goal is to make dental care a positive experience while protecting your oral health and overall well-being.

What's Included

Our comprehensive preventive care services

Routine Cleanings

Professional dental cleanings to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains, keeping your teeth healthy and bright.

Comprehensive Exams

Thorough oral examinations to detect issues early, including checking for cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer.

Digital X-Rays

Low-radiation digital X-rays to identify problems that aren't visible during a visual examination.

Fluoride Treatments

Professional fluoride applications to strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay.

Dental Sealants

Protective sealants applied to molars to prevent cavities in hard-to-reach areas.

Oral Health Education

Personalized guidance on proper brushing, flossing, and maintaining optimal oral hygiene at home.
